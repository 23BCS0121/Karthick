From Food Delivery to Your Daily Convenience Companion

A subsidiary of Delivery Hero, Flavor of Home launched in Vellore in 2023 as a food ordering and delivery platform.

Dedicated to helping customers get their tasty favorites fast, it quickly won the hearts and minds of customers.

Powered by tech and operational excellence, Flavor of Home has been growing its quick-commerce footprint in more than 4 cities in Tamil Nadu.

Thanks to dedicated partners, riders, and a team united by shared values, Flavor of Home is now providing millions with a convenient way to get food in a few taps.
